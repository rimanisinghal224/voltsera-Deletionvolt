Delete Your Voltsera Account

Voltsera allows users to locate nearby EV charging stations. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, you can request account deletion using the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion by sending an email to:

voltserainc@gmail.com

Please include the Google email address used to sign in to Voltsera.

Data Deletion

When your account is deleted:

  • Your Google account login information used in Voltsera will be removed.
  • Your app preferences and saved data will be deleted.
  • Any stored user-related data will be permanently removed from our systems.

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 days.
